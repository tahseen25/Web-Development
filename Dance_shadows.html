Dark Room

In a room so dark, with shadows stark,
A canvas stained, emotions parked.
Last thing you'd want, first thing to do,
Drowning in black, hiding the true.

Glass shards crushed, beneath a shoe,
Pain concealed, but it still grew.
Blood on the floor, a silent war,
Wounds unclean, infecting more.

Salt not sugar, the taste is bitter,
Lost in thoughts, can't find a glitter.
Down south we go, deep and wide,
A journey begun, no need to hide.

Chill in the air, abyss below,
Yet forward we move, courage to show.
Whispers from the abyss, in shadows we trust,
A tale unfolds, in the darkness thrust.
