Echoes in Stone

Upon the mountain, standing tall,
Grey-white building, proud in all.
A decade's tale within its walls,
From youth's mischief to wisdom's calls.

In every corner, memories reside,
Of art and stories, in it, I confide.
Through fall and rise, drama's spin,
A sanctuary where I found my kin.

Soulmates met in its embrace,
Lessons learned, a sacred space.
Whispers linger as I pass on by,
Long live the echoes, reaching the sky.

No longer its gates I tread,
Yet, in my heart, it's never fled.
A timeless bond, forever true,
Grey-white building, I salute to you.

As I stroll by, it whispers my name,
In every brick, my story's claim.
A symphony in stone, a lifelong friend,
"Echoes in Stone," the tale's sweet blend.
